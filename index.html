About this App

text2speech v2.2-PWA

Extracts text from images, PDFs, and DOCX documents in your browser using Tesseract.js v5, pdf.js, and Mammoth.js. Reads text aloud with Web Speech API.

New in v2.2: page markers between PDF/OCR pages, a selectable voice dropdown, and a collapsible ~200-word summary preview.

No data leaves your device — everything runs locally. Support: g.carruthers@rogers.com
